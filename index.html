Introduction to Chemistry

Chemistry is the branch of science that studies the composition, structure, and properties of matter. It explores how substances interact, combine, and change to form new materials.

Core Concepts of General Chemistry

1. Matter and Its Properties

Matter is anything that has mass and occupies space. It exists in different states: solid, liquid, and gas.

2. Atomic Structure

Atoms are the basic units of matter. They consist of protons, neutrons, and electrons that determine an element’s behavior.

3. The Periodic Table

The periodic table organizes all known elements by increasing atomic number and similar chemical properties.

4. Chemical Bonding

Atoms combine through ionic, covalent, or metallic bonds to form compounds with unique properties.

5. Chemical Reactions

In chemical reactions, substances transform into new products through breaking and forming of bonds.

Why Study Chemistry?

Chemistry connects the physical sciences with life sciences and applied fields. It helps us understand the world — from medicines and materials to energy and the environment.
